History Lesson

In 2012, TwentyOne Pilots signed on with Fueled Ramen, the same label that signed the likes of Fallout Boy, Panic at the Disco and Paramore. Their debut ablum was
Vessel in 2013 and the breakthrough success album Blurryface in 2015 which shot the duo to stardom. Since then the group has continued to put out chart topping singles
such as "Stressed Out", "Ride", and more recently "Heathens".
